LBO Mini-Model Builder

Enter the assumptions for any deal → get a full leveraged buyout model: sources & uses, projections, debt paydown, and MOIC / IRR returns.

Tip — saving & sharing a deal: this tool doesn’t remember your numbers after you close the tab. Click Export assumptions to save your current inputs as a small .json file (one per deal or scenario). Later, click Import assumptions and pick that file to reload everything exactly as it was — or email the file to a colleague so they see your numbers. The shared web link only opens a blank tool; the .json file is what carries a specific deal.
